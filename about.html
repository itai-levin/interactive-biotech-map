U.S. Biotech Visualizer

I put together this map as a way to visualize the distribution of biotech companies in the U.S.

The company names and descriptions were obtained from the amazing BioPharmGuy database.

I attempted to automatically retrieve company addresses to display them on the map. This process was highly imperfect. I likely missed many companies and retrieved outdated or incorrect addresses for others. The map is meant purely as a fun exercise and the accuracy or completeness of the data are by no way guaranteed.

If you found this useful, if you have ideas on how to make this more useful, or if you have any other comments or concerns, feel free to reach out on (fka) Twitter.
